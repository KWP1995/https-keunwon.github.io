Keunwon Park

Lyric Baritone

About

Keunwon Park is a Korean lyric baritone based in New York City. He studied at Seoul National University and is completing his Master of Music at Mannes School of Music. He has performed in numerous operatic and concert settings across Korea and the U.S.
